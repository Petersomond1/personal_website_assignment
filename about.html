MY NAME?

I bet; you've wondered, how-come am called "Monday" the name for the day of a week?

Well, my name is truly, MONDAY, hence am called Engr Monday-P (Monday Peters), am from the Ka of Afrika tribe also known as the ancient Holy City of the world.

.....And all those terms are actually of my language origin. And as you must have guessed rightly, I was named Monday partly because I was born on a Monday, and but more importantly, partly because that day and the other which humanity now know and used to name the days of the week; like monday was named after the passage day, or sacrficial day or worship day of one of the ancient king Gods of my ancestoral people. Monday was named after one of such king Gods of the Moors/Hebrewa/Nzeraeli/Jews/Engiuwa. So, first note that monday as a day itself originated as the special worship day of one of our ancient Gods; GODS of the nMoors (Ancient Nzeraele), which itself have the Moon as its totemic symbol of worship and anciently believed as the harbitation/route of that pantheom of the Gods, hence also called the Moon God or the nMo-ohoro-nNe i'Godo. And Peters as my Surname or my direct father's name, is not just Peter originating from the modern bible. No! My father, was named Peters after one of our ancient Gods called mKpi-Ita nMoo (that's Peter in my KA of Afrika tribal language); which literarily say/mean; the moulder of all herein that lives on this i'Ta (Earth) rock. So, you got it?
